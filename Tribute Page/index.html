Ademola Adeyoju

A technology attorney, certified privacy professional and Fellow in Information Privacy




Ademola Adeyoju is a technology attorney specialized in privacy and data protection, FinTech, cybersecurity, and intellectual property laws.


In 4+ years, he has advised blue-chip companies and government entities at one of the largest law firms in West Africa, worked at a data compliance organization, and managed the compliance department at CredPal, a former Y-Combinator and top Buy-Now-Pay-Later FinTech company in Nigeria.
As a certified privacy professional and Fellow in Information Privacy, he has worked with privacy laws of at least three continents: Europe, Africa, and North America.
